About Jabi Lake

Estimated Time Limit= 4 hrs


Jabi Lake is a water body formed from a man-made earth dam that was initially created to provide water to the residents of Abuja. The total surface area of the lake is about 1,300 hectares. The initial capacity of the reservoir was to supply water to 100,000b residents
